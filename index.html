- Interest rates (Fed policy) strongly affect valuations

- Aging population shifts healthcare demand
- ESG investing influences capital allocation
- Consumer behavior moving digital
- Wealth inequality affects spending patterns

⚙️ Technological

- AI and automation boost productivity
- Tech giants dominate index weighting
- Cybersecurity risks rising
- Innovation cycles drive growth sectors

🌱 Environmental

- Climate regulation impacts energy sector
- Transition to renewables reshapes industries
- ESG compliance becoming mandatory
- Natural disasters disrupt supply chains

⚖️ Legal

- SEC regulations ensure transparency
- Antitrust scrutiny on big tech
- Labor laws affect cost structures
- Compliance costs increasing
